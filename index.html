Company Overview

Earthblend Ventures is an independent climate advisory and carbon due diligence firm delivering high-integrity technical assessments for nature-based, waste management and clean-energy carbon projects across Africa and Asia.

Founded by Carbon Technical Expert Caroline Kimani, the firm brings over eight years of cross-continental experience in carbon accounting, project development, validation and verification, safeguards, and climate-strategy advisory.

Caroline Kimani has previously provided high-level technical expertise to international organisations including Mercy Corps, Nature Finance, Marubeni Corporation, UNCDF, and CAP-A, FSD Africa, FCDO, MasterCard Foundation and Rockefeller, delivering carbon strategy, feasibility assessments, biodiversity market insights, and investment opportunity screening across Africa.
