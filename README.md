# Earthblend Ventures

**Independent Climate Advisory & Carbon Due Diligence**

Delivering high-integrity technical assessments for nature-based, waste management and clean-energy carbon projects across Africa and Asia.

## About Us

Earthblend Ventures is an independent climate advisory and carbon due diligence firm providing rigorous technical evaluations for carbon and nature-based projects. Founded by Carbon Technical Expert **Caroline Kimani**, the firm brings over eight years of cross-continental experience in:

- Carbon accounting and project development
- Validation and verification
- Safeguards assessment
- Climate-strategy advisory

Caroline has previously provided technical expertise to leading international organizations including:
- Mercy Corps
- Nature Finance
- Marubeni Corporation
- UNCDF & CAP-A
- FSD Africa, FCDO
- MasterCard Foundation
- Rockefeller Foundation

## Core Services

### 1. **Carbon Due Diligence & Risk Assessment**
For REDD+, ARR, biochar, agroforestry, clean cooking, solar, waste management, and value-chain insetting projects.

### 2. **Independent Technical Reviews**
For investors and buyers covering methodology alignment, MRV robustness, geospatial analysis, leakage risks, safeguards, and credit-generation potential.

### 3. **Carbon Accounting & Impact Quantification**
Including GHG removals and emission-reduction modelling across nature-based and energy-access interventions.

### 4. **Stakeholder Engagement, FPIC & Safeguards**
Including benefit-sharing mechanism design and social risk assessments.

### 5. **Carbon Strategy & Policy Advisory**
Including input into national guidelines, investment pipelines, and accelerators.

### 6. **Biodiversity Market Scoping**
Nature-positive project analysis and market landscape assessments.
